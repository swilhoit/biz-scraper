Summary of E-Commerce Household Essentials Store

This turnkey e-commerce business specializes in household essentials, serving a steady and growing customer base with convenience and reliability. Generating approximately $120,000 in annual gross revenue and a net profit of $40,000, the business offers strong profitability with efficient operations managed in just 1015 hours per week.

The store operates on a 3PL fulfillment model, eliminating the need for warehousing or direct handling of inventory. This setup lowers overhead and allows for scalability without added complexity. The Shopify website is fully functional and professionally branded, supported by solid supplier and third-party logistics partnerships. Key digital assets include a customer email list, standard operating procedures, and a well-curated product line.

The household essentials market is stable and evergreen, providing consistent demand throughout the year. Despite competition from large retailers, this business differentiates itself through a curated product selection, reliable shipping, and excellent customer service. Growth opportunities abound, including expanding product offerings, launching paid advertising campaigns, and entering marketplaces like Amazon or Etsy. Additionally, there is potential to build email marketing and social media channels to boost repeat business.

The seller offers 30 days of comprehensive training and support to ensure a smooth transition. This business is ideally suited for entrepreneurs seeking a manageable, profitable online venture or investors looking to add a scalable e-commerce asset to their portfolio. With low fixed costs, minimal owner involvement, and clear paths for growth, this household essentials store represents a compelling acquisition.

Interested buyers are encouraged to inquire for detailed financials, operational insights, and next steps toward ownership.

Facilities & Assets
This business operates without a physical storefront or warehouse. All inventory is stored, packed, and shipped via a reliable 3PL (third-party logistics) provider. Market Outlook / Competition
The household essentials market is stable and in high demand. While competition exists, this store stands out with curated products, reliable 3PL fulfillment, and strong branding. Great growth potential via digital marketing and expanded product lines.
Opportunities for Growth
Growth opportunities include expanding product lines, increasing ad spend, launching on Amazon or Etsy, and building email/SMS marketing. The business has strong branding and systems in place. Challenges may include managing ad performance, supplier lead times, and keeping up with market trends.
